Thio-H

Advanced Systemic Insecticide

Thio-H is our premium systemic insecticide powered by advanced Thiamethoxam technology. Trusted by farmers nationwide, it delivers exceptional control of sucking pests, borers, and leaf miners through its unique systemic action. The water-dispersible granule formulation ensures superior plant uptake and extended protection for up to 15-20 days, maximizing crop yield potential while being safe for beneficial insects when used as directed.

Systemic Protection Plant-Safe Formula Long-Lasting Action

Key Benefits

  • Systemic Action

    Rapidly absorbed and distributed throughout the plant for comprehensive protection

  • Extended Protection

    Long-lasting control that reduces the need for frequent applications

  • Broad Spectrum Control

    Effective against multiple pest species including sucking pests and borers

  • Crop-Safe Formula

    Gentle on crops while being tough on target pests

Target Pests

Sucking Pests

Superior control of aphids, whiteflies, thrips, and other sap-feeding insects

Borers

Effective against stem borers, fruit borers, and shoot borers

Leaf Miners

Controls leaf mining insects that damage crop foliage

Usage & Safety Guidelines

Follow these professional recommendations for optimal results and safe handling

Application Guidelines

Recommended Dosage

200-250 ml per acre

Dilution Rate

Mix with 200 liters of water per acre for optimal coverage

Application Method

Foliar spray application with proper coverage

Timing & Frequency

Apply at first signs of pest infestation or as preventive measure

Safety Precautions

Personal Protection

Wear protective clothing, gloves, face mask, and eye protection during handling and application

Safe Handling

Wash hands thoroughly after handling. Keep away from food and feedstuffs

Re-Entry Period

Do not enter treated areas for 24 hours after application

Environmental Safety

Avoid application during high winds. Do not contaminate water bodies

Important Notice

Always read the product label and follow instructions carefully. The information provided here is general guidance. For specific crops and local conditions, consult with your agricultural advisor. Optimal application methods may vary by region and crop.

Frequently Asked Questions

Thio-H features advanced systemic action that provides both immediate and long-lasting protection. Its unique formulation ensures rapid uptake by plants, offering protection from within against various pests while being safe for beneficial insects when used as directed.

Thio-H works through dual action - systemic movement within the plant and contact activity. Once applied, it quickly enters the plant tissue, protecting new growth and providing extended control against target pests for up to 15-20 days.

Thio-H is extensively tested and proven safe for use on a wide range of crops including cotton, vegetables, fruits, and field crops when used according to label instructions. Its selective action targets harmful pests while being gentle on crops.
