Mode of action

How Profenofos and Cypermethrin pathways support reliable practical control.

  1. 1

    Profenofos pathway

    Profenofos inhibits acetylcholinesterase in insects, which interrupts nerve impulse transmission and reduces active feeding behavior.

  2. 2

    Cypermethrin pathway

    Cypermethrin affects sodium channels in nerve membranes, causing hyperexcitation, paralysis and eventual pest mortality.

  3. 3

    Combined field impact

    The dual chemistry helps in practical management of mixed infestations where both sucking and chewing pests are active together.

  4. 4

    Timing-first performance

    Better outcomes are achieved when application starts at threshold stage with calibrated spraying and full coverage discipline.

Usage best practices

Dose accuracy, premix method and timely spray execution.

  • 1

    Wear PPE before mixing

    Use gloves, mask, goggles and full-sleeve clothing while handling and spraying.

  • 2

    Measure dose accurately

    Use 30 ml per pump and avoid overdosing to maintain crop safety and responsible residue levels.

  • 3

    Prepare a premix first

    Mix measured quantity in a small water volume, then top up tank with continuous agitation.

Safety and responsibility

Protect operators, nearby crops and field ecology.

  • 4

    Spray in suitable weather

    Avoid high wind, peak heat and immediate rain windows to reduce drift and runoff loss.

  • 5

    Follow re-entry guidance

    Allow 24 hours before re-entry and ensure protective clothing for necessary field operations.

  • 6

    Store and dispose safely

    Keep away from food/feed and dispose containers according to label and local regulations.
