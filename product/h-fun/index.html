H-Fun

Premium Agricultural Insecticide for Comprehensive Pest Management

H-Fun is our specialized insecticide formulation engineered for targeted agricultural pest control. Trusted by professional farmers across India, it delivers exceptional control of sucking pests including aphids, whiteflies, and thrips with its advanced Imidacloprid 17.8% SL formulation. H-Fun provides rapid systemic action with visible results within 24-48 hours and offers prolonged protection for up to 14-21 days. With its superior translaminar movement, it effectively penetrates plant tissues to reach hidden pests, maximizing crop protection and yield potential across a wide range of crops.

H-Fun Usage & Safety Guidelines

Follow these professional recommendations for optimal results and safe handling

Application Guidelines

Recommended Dosage

125-150 ml per acre, depending on pest pressure and crop type

Dilution Rate

Mix with 200-250 liters of water per acre for optimal coverage

Application Method

Foliar spray using standard spray equipment with complete coverage

Timing & Frequency

Apply at first sign of pest infestation or as preventive measure. Repeat if necessary after 14-21 days.

Safety Precautions

Wear appropriate protective gear during mixing and application

Avoid contact with skin, eyes, and clothing

Store in cool, dry place away from direct sunlight

Keep out of reach of children and unauthorized persons

Important Notice

Always read the product label and follow instructions carefully. The information provided here is general guidance. For specific crops and local conditions, consult with your agricultural advisor. Optimal application methods may vary by region and crop.

Frequently Asked Questions about H-Fun

H-Fun begins working immediately after application, with visible results seen within 24-48 hours. Its systemic action ensures that the active ingredient is quickly absorbed and distributed throughout the plant, providing rapid control of target pests.

When used as directed, H-Fun has minimal impact on beneficial insects such as pollinators when applied during non-flowering stages. We recommend following integrated pest management practices and applying the product during early morning or late evening when beneficial insect activity is lower.

H-Fun provides effective pest control for 14-21 days after application under normal field conditions. The duration of protection may vary depending on pest pressure, environmental conditions, and crop growth rate.

H-Fun is compatible with most commonly used pesticides, fungicides, and foliar fertilizers. However, we recommend conducting a small compatibility test before mixing with other products. Always add H-Fun to the spray tank first, followed by other products.

Best Practices for Optimal Results

Application Timing

Apply H-Fun during early morning or late evening to minimize evaporation and maximize absorption. Avoid application during the hottest part of the day or when rain is expected within 6 hours.

Equipment Calibration

Ensure spray equipment is properly calibrated before application to achieve uniform coverage. Use appropriate nozzles that provide medium droplet size for optimal results.

Crop Monitoring

Regularly monitor crops for early signs of pest infestation. Early application at the first sign of pests ensures more effective control and prevents population buildup.
