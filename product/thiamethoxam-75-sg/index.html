Mode of action

How Thiamethoxam 75% SG works from the inside out to protect your crops.

  1. 1

    Rapid Absorption

    Thiamethoxam 75% SG dissolves instantly and is quickly taken up by the roots and foliage of the crop.

  2. 2

    Systemic Distribution

    It moves readily through the xylem, distributing the active ingredient uniformly to all plant parts including new growth.

  3. 3

    Nervous System Disruption

    Acts directly on the central nervous system of insects by interfering with nicotinic acetylcholine receptors.

  4. 4

    Feeding Cessation

    Affected insects rapidly stop feeding, become paralyzed, and eventually die, immediately halting crop damage.

Usage & safety guidelines

Ensure strict adherence to best practices for effective application and personal responsibility.

Application protocols

Steps to achieve superior, uniform pest control.

  • Use Proper PPE

    Always wear gloves, protective clothing, masks, and eye protection when handling the granules or spray mix.

  • Environmental Care

    Avoid spraying near water bodies. Do not spray during active bee foraging times to protect pollinators.

  • Storage Conditions

    Store in a cool, dry place, locked away from children, unauthorized persons, and animal feed.

Stewardship & protection

Secure operations and mitigate field risks.

  • Accurate Dosage

    Adhere precisely to the 10 gm per pump dosage to prevent resistance and ensure crop safety.

  • Equipment Hygiene

    Thoroughly wash the sprayer immediately after use. Empty containers must be triple-rinsed and disposed of safely.

  • First Aid Readiness

    In case of accidental ingestion or severe exposure, seek medical help immediately taking the product label along.
