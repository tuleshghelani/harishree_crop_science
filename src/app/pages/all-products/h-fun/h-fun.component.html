Frequently Asked Questions about H-Fun

Best Practices for Optimal Results

Application Timing

Apply H-Fun during early morning or late evening to minimize evaporation and maximize absorption. Avoid application during the hottest part of the day or when rain is expected within 6 hours.

Equipment Calibration

Ensure spray equipment is properly calibrated before application to achieve uniform coverage. Use appropriate nozzles that provide medium droplet size for optimal results.

Crop Monitoring

Regularly monitor crops for early signs of pest infestation. Early application at the first sign of pests ensures more effective control and prevents population buildup.
