PROCEED

Emamectin Benzoate 5% S G

Translaminar - Fast Knockdown - Long-lasting

Proceed features Emamectin Benzoate 5% S G in an advanced water-soluble granular formulation designed for robust caterpillar and pest control. Absorbed rapidly into leaf tissues via its translaminar action, it provides exceptional control of borers and leaf-eating caterpillars while remaining highly safe to beneficial insects. Ideal for protecting vital crops like cotton, cabbage, and chilli - backed by Harishree Crop Science quality.
