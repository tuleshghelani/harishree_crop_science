Usage & Safety Guidelines

Follow these professional recommendations for optimal results and safe handling

Application Guidelines

Recommended Dosage

150-200 ml per acre, depending on pest pressure and crop type

Dilution Rate

Mix with 200-250 liters of water per acre for optimal coverage

Application Method

Foliar spray using standard spray equipment with medium-sized droplets

Timing & Frequency

Apply at first sign of pest infestation. Repeat after 14-21 days if necessary, depending on pest pressure

Safety Precautions

Personal Protection

Always wear protective equipment including gloves, face mask, eye protection, and appropriate clothing during handling and application

Health Precautions

Avoid contact with eyes, skin, and inhalation. Wash hands thoroughly after handling. Do not eat, drink or smoke when using this product

Storage & Disposal

Store in original container away from direct sunlight, food, and feed. Keep at room temperature in a well-ventilated area. Triple rinse empty containers before disposal

General Safety

Keep out of reach of children and pets. Observe re-entry period of 24 hours after application. Follow local regulations for pesticide use

Important Notice

Always read the product label and follow instructions carefully. The information provided here is general guidance. For specific crops and local conditions, consult with your agricultural advisor. Optimal application methods may vary by region and crop.
