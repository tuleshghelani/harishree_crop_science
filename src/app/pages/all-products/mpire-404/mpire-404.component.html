Advanced Agricultural Insecticide for Superior Pest Control

MPIRE 404 is our premium high-concentration insecticide specifically formulated for professional agricultural pest management. Trusted by farmers nationwide, it delivers exceptional control of sucking pests like whiteflies, aphids, thrips, and jassids with its advanced Profenofos 40% + Cypermethrin 4% E.C. (Imidacloprid 40.4% SC) formulation. MPIRE 404 provides superior systemic protection for up to 21 days, ensuring thorough pest control even in challenging conditions. With its enhanced translaminar movement, it effectively reaches pests hiding on the undersides of leaves, maximizing crop protection and yield potential.
